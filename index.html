About Sebastian.

Sebastian Rangel, born and raised in Cali, Colombia, came to the United States to pursue his dream of becoming a media designer. He is dedicated to honing his skills in both coding and design. He is ready to bring your ideas to life through empathy and a deep understanding of your brand vision.

Philosophy

As a media designer, I believe that design is not merely about aesthetics but about creating meaningful connections between ideas and audiences. My Philosophy centers on the idea that every project, whether it's a website, a brand identity, or a multimedia presentation, should tell a story that resonates on an emotional level. I strive to blend creativity with functionality, ensuring that my designs are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and accessible. I am inspired by the constantly evolving nature of media and technology, and I embrace innovation while staying grounded in timeless design principles. Ultimately, my goal is to craft experiences that engage, inspire, and leave a lasting impact.

New Media Technology

New media technology revolutionizes how we interact and consume information, shaping industries and societies. UX/UI design, coding, and digital design are crucial for creating seamless experiences and driving innovation in this digital age, enhancing productivity and connectivity for the future.

Packaging Design

Packaging design is paramount to me. It's the face of a brand, the first impression that captivates consumers. I see it as a fusion of creativity and strategy, where every detail speaks volumes about the product within.

Logo

Logo design is essential as it serves as the visual cornerstone of a brand, swiftly conveying its essence and character. It's about crafting a memorable mark that sparks recognition and leaves a lasting impression in the minds of consumers.
